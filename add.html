Just so you know, tag spam in titles does not work! You are free to tag spam, as although my algorithm will show websites with the highest number of matched keywords first, it also shows shorter titles before longer ones to prevent tag spam! If you tag spam, you may be shown more often, but you will appear much further down the list than others! People are also unlikely to click on tag spam titles!


Enter the title of the webpage - short titles that contain keywords users would search for make great titles the algorithm favours, meaning they will show higher up the list!


Enter the URL of the webpage - this is the website address that the user will be taken to when they click on the result. For example, https://Searchly.123programming.repl.co.


Enter the description of the webpage - fairly short descriptions that contain keywords users would search for make great descriptions the algorithm favours, meaning they will show higher up the list!
